# Automotive Sales Performance Dashboard (End-to-End Analysis)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive data analysis for an automotive distributor, aimed at monitoring sales performance, profitability, and inventory efficiency across multiple branches and car brands. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights to optimize revenue and operational strategies.

## 📊 Key Business Insights
- **Revenue & Profitability:** Achieved a total revenue of **$4.02bn** with a net profit of **$472.8M** (Profit Margin: **11.77%**).
- **Sales Growth:** Observed a significant growth in both Profit (**+24.32%**) and Quantity (**+24.94%**) compared to the previous period.
- **Brand Performance:** **Chery**, **Hyundai**, and **Nissan** are the top-performing brands by quantity sold.
- **Branch Analysis:** The **Cairo - Nasr City** branch is the leading contributor to both revenue ($1.61bn) and profit ($190M).
- **Payment Methods:** **Cash** transactions dominate the sales volume ($1.66bn), followed by Bank Finance and Installments.
- **Inventory Efficiency:** Average days in stock is **59.62 days**, providing a clear metric for inventory turnover.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills
- **Power BI:** Dashboard visualization and interactive reporting.
- **Power Query:** Data cleaning and transformation (ETL).
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Created advanced measures for Profit Margin, Growth KPIs (YoY/LQ), and Average Profit per Unit.
- **Data Modeling:** Implemented a **Star Schema** involving:
  - **Fact Table:** `Fact_Sales`
  - **Dimension Tables:** `Dim_Cars`, `Dim_Branches`, `Dim_Employees`, and `Dim_Date`.

## 📂 Dashboard Structure
The report consists of three main analytical perspectives:
1. **Sales Overview:** High-level KPIs including Revenue, COGS, and Profit trends over time (2025-2026).
2. **Product & Inventory Analysis:** Detailed breakdown by Car Model, Segment (Mid, Economic, Luxury), and Origin (Imported vs. Local).
3. **Branch & Employee Performance:** Comparison of revenue and quantity growth across different regions and sales representatives.

## 📈 Visualizations Highlights
- **Trend Analysis:** Area charts showing revenue fluctuations by month.
- **Comparative Analysis:** Bar charts for Brand and Model performance.
- **Geographic/Branch Distribution:** Treemaps for revenue contribution by branch.
- **Segment Breakdown:** Donut charts for quantity distribution by car segments.
